About Heritage Bengal

Celebrating Bengal's rich heritage through exquisite gold plated jewellery

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Heritage Bengal was born from a deep appreciation for Bengali culture and craftsmanship. We believe that every piece of jewellery should tell a story, carry tradition, and create lasting memories.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to make the exquisite beauty of Bengali traditional jewellery accessible to modern jewelry lovers while maintaining the authenticity and elegance of our heritage.

Each piece in our collection is inspired by the rich artistic traditions of Bengal, from the intricate patterns of traditional folk art to the timeless elegance of classical Bengali jewelry designs.

Our Mission

To preserve and celebrate Bengali heritage through contemporary jewelry designs that blend traditional craftsmanship with modern elegance, making every moment special for our customers.
